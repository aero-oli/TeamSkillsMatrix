Getting Started

Logging In

To access the Skills Matrix:

  1. Enter your email address
  2. Enter your password
  3. Optional: Check "Remember me" to stay logged in for 30 days
  4. Click "Sign In"
Viewing Your Skills

On the main dashboard, you can:

  • See your current skill levels
  • Check training certification status
  • Monitor expiry dates for required training
  • View your detailed skill report
  • Read and add notes for each skill
Updating Your Skills

To update your skill levels:

  1. Click "Update Skills" on your profile
  2. Set proficiency levels (1-5 stars)
  3. Add training dates where required
  4. Add notes or comments about your experience
  5. Click "Save Changes"
Training Records

Managing your training records:

  • View required training for each skill
  • Check training expiry dates
  • Update training completion dates
  • Monitor certification status
  • Add notes about training experiences

Administrative Functions

Employee Management Tab

Managing employees and admin users:

  • View and manage admin users
  • Add new employees with their details
  • Assign projects and levels
  • Update employee information
  • View comprehensive skill reports
  • Search and filter employee records
  • Manage employee permissions
Skills Management Tab

Managing the skills database:

  • Add new skills with descriptions
  • Set training requirements
  • Configure expiry periods
  • Update existing skills
  • Search and filter skills
  • View skill requirements and training details
Options Management Tab

Organizing the team structure:

  • Create and manage projects
  • Define employee levels/grades
  • Set level ordering
  • Assign employees to projects
  • Configure organizational hierarchy
Tools Tab

System maintenance and backup:

  • Download complete database backups
  • Restore system from backup files
  • Manage system data
  • Perform database maintenance
Important: Always create a backup before making significant changes to the system.
